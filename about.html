About

SoulFa 靈魂沙發

"Like sinking into a cozy sofa after a long day, all by yourself, free-spirited; a corner reserved only for the wildest of dreams."

·

SoulFa 靈魂沙發，成立於 2018 年的台北，目前成員為主唱兼吉他手本山、吉他手 Poopoo 和貝斯手亨利。他們的音樂風格深受 90 年代的英倫搖滾啟蒙，並加入草根搖滾、迷幻搖滾等元素，創作語彙則涵蓋華語及英語，至今發行過三張專輯、一張 EP 及無數首單曲。

他們歌頌著對於世界與人際之間的觀察，試圖用創作真空壓縮生活中的所有雜念，同時有收納靈魂之含義，讓樂迷能在音樂裡深呼吸，尤其是充滿魔幻感的歌詞，搭配猶如暢遊在公路電影般的旋律，造就樂團獨有的特質。

SoulFa (靈魂沙發), whose Mandarin name stands for "sofa with a soul", was founded in 2018 in Taipei. The trio—lead vocalist/guitarist Panson (本山), guitarist Poopoo, and bassist Henry (亨利)—crafts a signature sound that blends 90's Britpop with elements of roots rock and psychedelic rock, their lyrics flowing between Mandarin and English.

Having released three acclaimed albums and numerous singles while selling out multiple shows since their first album tour, SoulFa has quickly established themselves as a prominent force in Taiwan's indie scene.

Through their songwriting, SoulFa celebrates the intricacies of human connection. Their dreamy lyrics and smooth-sailing melodies, reminiscent of classic road movies, offer an escape from daily clutters, inviting audiences to pause, breathe, and find spiritual solace within the music's embrace.
