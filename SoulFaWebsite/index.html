01 — About

Three guys, one couch,
a lot of unfinished thoughts.

SoulFa is a Taipei-based indie rock trio formed in 2018, drawing from 90's Britpop, roots rock, and psychedelic rock. We write in both Mandarin and English, often about the strange feeling of growing up too fast in a city that never sleeps.

2025 年底，我們發行了第三張專輯《失望的山》——關於翻過那座山，回頭看看自己的故事。
